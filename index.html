Allen came to our planet because of the abundance of cheese resources we have. He comes here in peace, and has the ability to instantly transform milk into cheese. therefore he has made friends with many cows.

Allen doesnt mind sharing his cheese, the farmers have warmed up to him and come to an agreement that he can make cheese as long as they get 3/4 of what he produces.

Allen wants to stay - the town say's No way!
